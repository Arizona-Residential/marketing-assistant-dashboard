Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the AI Marketing Assistant (“we,” “our,” or “us”) collects, uses, and protects information when you connect your social media accounts.

Information We Collect

  • Account identifiers (such as TikTok user ID) and basic profile data when you connect a social account.
  • Content analytics such as views, likes, comments, shares, and post metadata.

How We Use Information

  • To display analytics and performance insights inside the dashboard.
  • To generate recommendations to improve content performance.

Data Storage

Data is stored locally on your device and not shared with third parties. OAuth tokens are stored securely in a local database file.

Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Contact
